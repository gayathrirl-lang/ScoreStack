## 🎬 3-MINUTE RECORDING SEQUENCE

### [0:00 – 0:35] Step 1: The Loan Officer Pipeline & AI Decision Engine
* **Screen:** `login_screen` → `lo_dashboard_v2` → `lo_company_v2`
* **Action:** 
  1. Start on the login page. Click **"Enter Loan Officer Portal"**.
  2. Briefly gesture to the **"All Applications"** list in the sidebar.
  3. Under the *Sharma Textiles* row in the main view, click **"🔍 Open Analysis"**.
  4. **PAUSE** and let the 5-step AI Decision Engine animation play completely.
* **Point To:** The changing steps in the AI Engine (Identity Validation → Trad Credit → Feature Engineering → Score Reveal).
* **Say:** 
  > "Welcome to ScoreStack AI for IDBI Bank. Let's step into the shoes of a Loan Officer. When an application for Sharma Textiles arrives, our AI Decision Engine instantly triggers. In 10 seconds, it evaluates identity, pulls traditional credit data, and performs live feature engineering. What used to take a human underwriter three weeks of manual verification is now synthesized into a highly accurate, explainable credit score instantly."
* **Proves:** 60-second Decisioning & Operational Efficiency.

### [0:35 – 1:05] Step 2: Explainable Underwriting (SHAP) & Legal Checks
* **Screen:** `lo_company_v2` (Sharma Textiles) → `lo_company_v2` (Metro Builders)
* **Action:** 
  1. After Sharma Textiles loads, scroll down slightly to point out the **AI Credit Story** paragraph and the **"Drivers (SHAP Value)"** waterfall chart.
  2. In the left sidebar under *Demo companies*, select **Metro Builders**.
  3. Once it loads, click the **"⚖️ Legal, Compliance & MCA"** tab.
* **Point To:** The **"🚨 IBBI Insolvency Proceeding Active"** red warning box on the Metro Builders profile.
* **Say:** 
  > "For every score, ScoreStack generates an LLM-driven AI Credit Story and a SHAP waterfall chart, ensuring total explainability for regulatory compliance. But the real power is risk prevention. Let's look at Metro Builders. ScoreStack automatically queries MCA21 and e-Courts in the background, instantly flagging an active IBBI insolvency proceeding, providing a critical 12-month early warning on potential NPAs before the loan is ever disbursed."
* **Proves:** Explainability, 12-month NPA Warning, RBI Compliance.

### [1:05 – 1:35] Step 3: Financial Inclusion for NTC (New-To-Credit)
* **Screen:** `lo_company_v2` (Ravi Electricals NTC)
* **Action:** 
  1. In the left sidebar, select **Ravi Electricals (NTC)**.
  2. The AI Decision Engine will play again—this time highlighting **"Alternate Data Intelligence Engine"** and the 8 proxy signals.
  3. Once the dashboard loads, point out the **"NTC"** badge and the high score.
* **Point To:** The "Alternate Data Engine" steps during loading, and the Account Aggregator/UPI data metrics on the dashboard.
* **Say:** 
  > "What about the 65% of MSMEs that are credit-invisible? Let's evaluate Ravi Electricals. Because they lack CIBIL and GST history, ScoreStack seamlessly routes them through our Alternate Data Engine. By analyzing 18 months of utility bill punctuality, Account Aggregator cash flows, and UPI transaction momentum, we successfully generate a highly reliable credit proxy—safely bringing New-To-Credit businesses into the formal IDBI ecosystem."
* **Proves:** NTC Inclusion via Alternate Data (UPI/Utility).

### [1:35 – 2:15] Step 4: Business Owner Experience & FutureLens™
* **Screen:** `bo_owner_select` → `bo_score_screen`
* **Action:** 
  1. Click **"🔙 Switch Portal"** in the sidebar.
  2. Click **"Enter Business Owner Portal"**.
  3. Click the tile for **Rajesh Sharma**.
  4. On the Sharma Textiles dashboard, click the **"🔮 FutureLens™ Risk Projections"** tab.
  5. Drag the "GST filing regularity" and "UPI inflow" sliders to the right. 
  6. Click **"⚡ Run AI Simulation"** and watch the projected score jump.
* **Point To:** The interactive sliders and the "Projected Score" metric updating in real time.
* **Say:** 
  > "Now, let's flip to the borrower's perspective. When Rajesh Sharma logs in via single-click Aadhaar, he doesn't just see a static score—he sees actionable intelligence. Under the FutureLens tab, Rajesh can use our AI simulator to ask 'What if?'. If he improves his GST filing regularity and increases UPI adoption, he can instantly see how it boosts his IDBI creditworthiness, gamifying financial health and coaching him toward better banking behavior."
* **Proves:** Customer Engagement, Gamification, AI Simulator.

### [2:15 – 3:00] Step 5: ScoreStack AI CFO Chat & Outro
* **Screen:** `bo_score_screen` (AI CFO Tab)
* **Action:** 
  1. Click the **"💬 ScoreStack AI CFO"** tab.
  2. Click one of the pre-filled prompt buttons like *"How can I improve my score to get a lower interest rate?"*

* **Point To:** The chat interface generating contextual, personalized financial advice.
* **Say:** 
  > "Finally, to prevent MSMEs from feeling abandoned after a rejection or confused by a score, every borrower gets a 24/7 personalized AI CFO. This GenAI assistant understands their exact financial twin and provides tailored, actionable advice on improving their credit standing. 
  > 
  > ScoreStack AI isn't just an underwriting tool—it's a complete ecosystem that slashes decision times, prevents NPAs, and unlocks credit for millions of MSMEs. Thank you for your time."
* **Proves:** Contextual GenAI, Reduced Customer Acquisition Cost, IDBI Innovation.
